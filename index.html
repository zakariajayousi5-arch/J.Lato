About J.Lato

Where gelato meets café culture

J.Lato is built around the little rituals people love — a creamy scoop after dinner, a coffee break with friends, a late-night affogato, or a dessert that turns into the best part of the day.

The menu brings together artisanal gelato, espresso-based coffee, indulgent shakes, and signature specials with a playful personality. It’s warm, inviting, and made for people who take dessert seriously.
